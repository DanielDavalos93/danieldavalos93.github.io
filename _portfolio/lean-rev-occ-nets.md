A **Lean Mechanization of Reversible Occurrence Nets** is a project written in [Lean Prover](lean-lang.org) with fully mechanization of definitions and with proved theorems (without `sorry`'s), mainly base on the [Reversing Place Transition Nets](https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.04266) paper.

Useful links for the project
------
- Blueprint (website): <https://danieldavalos93.github.io/reversible-occ-nets/>
- Open repository: <https://github.com/danieldavalos93/reversible-occ-nets/>
- [Dependency graphs](https://danieldavalos93.github.io/reversible-occ-nets/blueprint/dep_graph_document.html): The square light green nodes represent _definitions_, the circle (dark green, light green and white) nodes represent _lemmas_ and _theorems_.

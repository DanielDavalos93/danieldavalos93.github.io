Since march until july, and 2 hs of theory and 3 hs of practical exercises, the topics were:

- Turing machine (deterministic and non-deterministic)
- Computing time
- Polynomial time:
  - **P** class
  - **NP** class
- Polynomial reduction and **NP-complete** problems
  - Cook-Levin theorem
  - The **co-NP** class
  - The **ExpTime** and **NExpTime**
- Separation of complexity class
  - Deterministic time hierarchies
  - Non-deterministic time hierarchies
  - Leadner theorem
- Space used by a computation
  - Polynomial space: **PSpace** and **NPSpace**
  - Savitch theorem
  - Logarithmic space: **L** and **NL**
  - Immerman-Szelepcsényi theorem
- Polynomial hierarchy
  - $\Sigma_i^p$-**complete** problems
  - Oracle machines
  - Bacher-Gill-Solovay theorem
- Boolean circuits
  - The **P**$_{/poly}$ class
  - Karp-Lipton theorem
  - **NC** and **AC** classes
  - **P**-completeness
